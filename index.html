PulseTogether Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Introduction

PulseTogether is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Information We Collect

We collect health and fitness data you choose to share through connected services:

  • Apple Health (steps, heart rate, sleep, workouts)
  • WHOOP (recovery, strain, HRV, sleep)
  • Fitbit (activity, heart rate, sleep)

We only access data you explicitly authorize.

How We Use Your Information

Your health data is used to display your metrics and share selected data with friends/circles you choose. We do not sell your personal health data to third parties.

Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Your Rights

You can disconnect any health service at any time through app settings, and revoke permissions through your device settings.

Contact

Questions? Contact us through the app's support features.
